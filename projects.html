Elections newsletters

For class, a group of four, including myself, wrote weekly newsletters im the four weeks that lead up to the General Municipal Election in Columbia, Mo. We also wrote the newsletters the day of and included a results follow-up. We used the articles written by reporters to find issues readers would be interested to know about, including a FAQ section at the bottom of the page.
